Raiden is a scrolling shooter arcade game developed and published by Seibu Kaihetsu in 1990.

The objective is to advance towards the end of each screen killing or avoinding enemies in order to arrive to the boss fight and after killing it advancing to the next level. Although the game has an ending, after beating it the game restarts with the it's difficulty increased.



The plot of the game takes place in 2090 when planet Earth is attacked by the alien race Cranassians and in order to defeat them the Wolrd Alliance Military builds a new aircraft based on an alien one to fight back, the Raiden Supersonic Attack Fighter.
